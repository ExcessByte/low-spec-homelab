# The Hardware
So the hardware is pretty... Old. I know that's why started this but it is really old. Atleast it supports 64 bit

The laptop is a Wipro LittleGenius. The body was already breaking down and the CPU cooler is dead. Laptop CPU fans cost too much and I didn't want to buy something for this this laptop. SO I thought I will make a new case for it.

Turns out I can't build something that looks good.

I just took a piece of soft wood and cut it to fit the main mother board and also the length of the hard disk.

I didnt want to make the board rest on the components and put stress on the board unevenly. So i cut some rectangles and made some stand offs. in places where there were scres already.

I didn't quite like the design, I wanted to make it compact and small but it is back to the laptop dimensions because of the hard drive sticking out. I didn't want it to stay hanging. Also I remove every part and dissasembled everything. Including the display, laptops trackpad, keyboard, etc. I think the wifi card was also dead because it was not being detected in any of the distos. I wanted the least amount of power consumtopn so i am just running the bare mother board connected to the internet via the ethernect cable directly connected to the router.

I am fixing the mother board to the standoffs with push pins because 1) I dont have the screws and 2) I am lazy( I had to remove it everytime I want to debug because the display cable connector is on the under side of the motherboard).
